The Ultimate Guide to Removing a Building Violation in New York City

Ignoring building violations can have serious consequences. The DOB can issue fines, order the property to be vacated, or even demolish the property. In addition, property owners with outstanding violations may have difficulty obtaining financing or selling their property.

Building violations can be a headache for property owners. Not only can they lead to fines, but they can also make it difficult to sell or refinance your property. If you're facing building violations, there are a few things you can do to get them removed.

How to Remove a Building Violation

There are a few ways to remove a building violation. You can:

  • Correct the violation and submit proof to the DOB.
  • Request a hearing with the DOB to contest the violation.
  • Hire a violation removal services company to help you get the violation dismissed.
